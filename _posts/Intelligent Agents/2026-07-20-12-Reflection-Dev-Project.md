The design phase of the project did not go well. Team members were not active on the forum. There was no live meeting where all team members were present. One team member did all design and shared the design proposal through WhatsApp. I provided feedback on the design at this stage, which was partly incorporated, and made improvements to the proposal. Then another team member rewrote the proposal almost completely. It was an improvement, but did not feel collaborative.

If I had installed WhatsApp sooner, maybe things would have gone better, but I don’t think I should need a third party communication platform for group work.

I found the implementation phase better, but still not optimal. Due to the commitments involved with working full-time and being a new parent, I struggled to find time for development on top of the other writing the course required. Eventually, I installed a coding assistant and was able to make progress. I wish I had leaned on the assistant a bit less so that I could understand more. However, I believe I was able to maintain the same professional standards I would apply at my job for such a prototype.

Overall, the project was interesting and a good test of basic development principles. I probably should have read the week 11 assignment deliverables as soon as the proposal was submitted, and started work then. Focusing on the writing tasks above all else unfortunately resulted in a development rush.
