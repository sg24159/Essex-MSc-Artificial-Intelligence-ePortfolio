As I noted in my first discussion post, the emergent behaviours of agents has long been an area of concern in the field of intelligent agents. Multi-agent systems in particular can be hard to simulate, and therefore unpredictable (Marik and McFarlane, 2005; Leitão, Mařík and Vrba, 2013). Now, with non-deterministic LLM based agents becoming the norm, the hard to predict nature of multi-agent systems also applies to individual agents (An, 2026). When LLMs are combined into multi-agent systems, they exhibit emergent behaviours not seen in isolation, which must be understood and tested for safe deployment (Erisken et al., 2025).
